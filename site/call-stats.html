Barstow Area Call Stats

SBSO Barstow and CHP Barstow activity without double-counting revisions.

This dashboard reads the public call log, groups revision suffixes into unique incidents, and focuses on Barstow-area sheriff activity plus CHP Barstow station incidents. SBSO Barstow-area currently means SBSO records with station "Barstow" or a BA call-number prefix.
