How to read this page

Blue rows are SBSO, darker SBSO rows are still open, deeper blue rows are CHP, and orange rows are fire/medical responses.

The `+` button opens revision history, current details, and any extra structured data attached to that call.

Call number colors follow prefix, arrest dispositions are boxed red, and the area tag is split from the street address.

Use the prefix filter, date range, or typeable column filters to narrow the feed, then export the matched rows to CSV or JSON.
